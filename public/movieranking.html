The Best Movies According To Me

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Kimi No nawa

A beautiful storyline and great animations.

Ready Player One

You will definitely fall in love with the plot, the characters and the CGI. Overall a great freakin movie.

Spiderman- Across the spider-verse

Breathtaking animations and character design. It makes that connection between the audience and the characters that so many other movies fail to make. The ending might leave you wanting more.
